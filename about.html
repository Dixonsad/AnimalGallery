About the Animal Gallery

The Animal Gallery is a static website designed to showcase some of the most fascinating creatures on Earth. We aim to provide interesting facts and beautiful images to spark curiosity and appreciation for wildlife especially those that are endangered.

Our gallery includes animals from various habitats and provides insights into their lifestyles, diets, and conservation statuses. Enjoy exploring the amazing world of animals!
